About Us

The Iman Jibril Charity Foundation was founded in 2022 by Mrs. Ikram Ahmed in honor of her children, Iman and Jibril. What began as a mother’s personal mission is now a continent-wide movement to restore hope, dignity, and opportunity for underserved communities. From its roots in the Eastern Region of Ghana, the Foundation has grown into a force for change building schools, clinics, and programs that touch thousands of lives.

Inspired by Ikram’s own journey from refugee child to global entrepreneur, our work is a living legacy of resilience and compassion. We don’t do charity as usual. We build for generations.

360° Community Development Programme

By confronting today’s most urgent challenges, Iman Jibril Foundation employs a comprehensive, multifaceted strategy to strengthen communities in need. This full-circle development programme is devoted to breaking the persistent cycle of hunger, poverty and unemployment throughout Africa and the world at large.

Our Programs

Feeding Programme

Our Feeding Programme provides nutritious meals to children and families in need, ensuring no one goes hungry. We partner with local communities to deliver food support and promote healthy living.

Water Supply Solutions

We implement sustainable water projects, including wells and filtration systems, to provide clean and safe water to underserved communities, reducing waterborne diseases and improving quality of life.

Women Empowerment

Our Women Empowerment initiatives offer skills training, microfinance, and mentorship, enabling women to become leaders and entrepreneurs in their communities.

Education

We build and support schools, provide scholarships, and supply learning materials to ensure every child has access to quality education and a brighter future.

Vocational Training

Our vocational programs equip youth and adults with practical skills for employment and entrepreneurship, breaking the cycle of poverty.

Agricultural Sustainability

We promote sustainable farming techniques and provide resources to help communities achieve food security and environmental stewardship.

Health Care

Our health initiatives include medical outreach, clinics, and health education to improve well-being and save lives in vulnerable communities.
